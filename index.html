What is Coolio?
Coders vs Designers, Thinkers vs Doers: Who hasn’t been stuck in a very heated meeting, that never seems to end?
How does it work?
Coolio detects the “heatscore” of your meeting. It measures the sound volume of the conversation, the sentiment via Watson’s sentiment recognition - and even the air quality in the room via The Things Network!
And then what?
Heatscore Orange: The lights in your room will start to blink as a first warning
Heatscore Red: Coolio will lower the room temperature to literally cool down the discussion
Heatscore Lockdown: Your office gets notified to deliver the first aid Coolio Kit: Beer!
